# TripMate Project Brief

## One-Liner

TripMate helps people turn trip ideas into practical, collaborative itineraries.

## Initial Audience

- Friends or couples planning leisure trips together
- Solo travelers comparing many saved places
- Weekend and short-trip planners who care about time, cost, and route realism

## MVP Slice

1. Create a trip with destination and dates.
2. Add saved places with notes and priority.
3. Organize places into itinerary days.
4. Show simple time, cost, and movement context.
5. Share a read-only plan or invite companions later.

## Near-Term Decisions

- Data persistence: local-first demo, then database.
- Maps provider: choose only when route and place features need it.
- Auth: defer until collaboration requires real accounts.
- AI features: start with itinerary suggestions only after core manual planning works.

## Open Questions

- Will TripMate focus first on Korea domestic travel, international travel, or both?
- Should the first MVP prioritize mobile planning or desktop planning?
- Are bookings tracked manually, through integrations, or both?
